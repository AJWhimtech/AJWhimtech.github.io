Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 8, 2025

🔒 Our Privacy-First Promise

AJ Whimtech LLC is committed to protecting your privacy. Our apps are designed with privacy as a core feature - we intentionally minimize data collection and prioritize your digital privacy.

Interpretation and Definitions

Definitions

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy:

  • "Company" (referred to as either "the Company", "we", "us" or "our" in this Agreement) refers to AJ Whimtech LLC, Ohio, USA.
  • "Device" means any device that can access the Service such as a computer, a cell phone or a digital tablet.
  • "Personal Data" is any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
  • "Service" refers to our mobile applications and website.
  • "Usage Data" refers to data collected automatically, either generated by the use of the Service or from the Service infrastructure itself.
  • "You" means the individual accessing or using the Service.

Information We Collect and Use

What We Do NOT Collect

  • No account creation or login required
  • No personal information (names, emails, addresses, phone numbers)
  • No user-generated content stored on our servers
  • No behavioral tracking or analytics
  • No location data or geolocation information
  • No biometric information
  • No financial or payment information

Limited Technical Information We May Collect

We may automatically receive minimal technical information necessary for app functionality and improvement:

  • Crash Reports: Basic technical data to identify and fix bugs (no personal information included)
  • App Store Analytics: General usage statistics provided by app stores (download counts, general device types)
  • Device Information: Basic device type and operating system version (for compatibility purposes only)

Tracking Technologies and Cookies

Our mobile applications do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies. Our website may use minimal cookies for basic functionality:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality (session management)
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising

How We Use Information

The limited technical information we may collect is used solely for:

  • Maintaining and improving app functionality and stability
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Understanding general usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Ensuring app compatibility across different devices

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Since we don't collect personal information, there is minimal information to share. We do not sell, trade, or rent any user information. Limited technical data may be shared only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate our apps (crash reporting services, app stores)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to respond to valid legal requests
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (though no personal data would be transferred)

Data Retention

We retain minimal technical data only as long as necessary:

  • Crash reports: Retained to resolve any underlying issues with the applications
  • App store analytics: As provided by app store platforms
  • Support communications: Retained as needed for customer communication

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect any information we might receive against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Special Protection for Young Children

Our apps are specifically designed for young children although anyone can enjoy them. We provide enhanced privacy protection:

  • Zero data collection from children of any age
  • No account creation, registration, or personal information required
  • No communication features or ability to share information online
  • All app content and creations remain on the device
  • No advertising or third-party content
  • Parents can safely allow use without privacy concerns

We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by design - we simply don't collect any personal information from users of any age.

Your Privacy Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, most data rights don't apply. However:

  • Data Portability: All app content is stored locally on your device and belongs to you
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all local data
  • Access: You can contact us for information about any support communications
  • Opt-out: Users may choose to not use our services

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have specific rights. Since we don't collect personal information as defined by CCPA, most provisions don't apply. However:

  • We do not sell personal information
  • We do not discriminate based on privacy choices
  • You can request information about our data practices

Categories of Information (CCPA Disclosure)

In the past 12 months, we have not collected personal information in the categories defined by CCPA. The only technical information we may collect does not constitute "personal information" under CCPA as it cannot identify individuals.

International Data Transfers

Since we don't collect personal data, there are no personal data transfers. Any technical information is processed in the United States where our company is located.

Do Not Track

Our mobile applications do not respond to Do Not Track signals because we don't track users. Our website does not track users across third-party websites.

Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

    • In some cricumstances we may also provide notice through our Service
